Data is sent through your home devices through a wifi connection using IP address which routes information to a ISP (Internet Service Provider) which sends packets of information.
DNS manages the sending and recieving of web files and IP is the breakdown and transport of packets.
cookies are small pieces of data sent from different websites and stored on your computer by the specific web browser.
URL or Uniform Resourse Locator has three basic parts which include protocol, domain name, and path.
